Breakout & Retest Indicator
Pine Script v5 – Detects potential breakout and retest opportunities.

Description:
This indicator automatically identifies key support and resistance levels based on the highest highs and lowest lows over the last 20 bars. It highlights potential breakout and retest setups with visual labels:

Buy Signal: Price breaks above support, retests the level, and closes above previous bar.

Sell Signal: Price breaks below resistance, retests the level, and closes below previous bar.

How to Use:

Add the script to your TradingView chart.

The indicator plots support (green) and resistance (red) lines automatically.

Look for BUY or SELL labels at retests as potential entries.

⚠ Important:

Signals should not be used standalone.

Always confirm with other technical filters such as:

Price action patterns

Trend direction

Volume analysis

Supply/demand zones

Backtest thoroughly before live trading.
